About TypeTogether

TypeTogether is a communal digital typewriter where your words join an endless, shared narrative.

Each keystroke you make becomes part of a collaborative story, visible to everyone who visits this page. Continue where others left off, or start fresh with a new page.

How it works: Your typing is automatically saved and synchronized. Other writers can read and add to your words, creating an ever-evolving tapestry of thoughts, stories, and ideas.
